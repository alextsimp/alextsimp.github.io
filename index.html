Featured Research & Engineering Projects

Advanced Thermal Storage Systems

Contributing to major EU Horizon projects (MINISTOR, THUMBSUP, RE-WITCH) to unlock grid balancing potential and optimize industrial waste heat.

  • Modelling of a novel thermal storage system in ASPEN and MATLAB/SIMULINK.
  • Developed thermodynamic and dynamic models for absorption chillers and thermal storage using MATLAB, maximizing operational reliability by 10%.
  • Conducted waste heat analysis for a biogas plant, identifying recovery potential to enhance energy efficiency by 20%.
MATLAB/SIMULINK ASPEN TRNSYS

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)

Developing and optimizing kinetic codes applied to microfluidics, rarefied gas dynamics, and vascular therapies (MAGNA project).

  • Development of an in-house computational fluid particle dynamics code.
  • Simulation of aerosol flows with ANSYS FLUENT.
  • Simulation of magnetic nanocarriers dispersion within blood flow for intravascular drug delivery.
FORTRAN Python ANSYS FLUENT COMSOL

Sustainable Urban Energy Design

Driving positive energy and resilient city initiatives through the RESPONSE EU Horizon project and municipal design.

  • Utilized RETSCREEN to design and analyze municipal buildings, converting them into positive energy buildings.
  • Performed BIM and process modeling using TRNSYS to design sustainable energy solutions.
  • Processed and analyzed energy consumption data for use in an in-house LCA software to assess environmental and sustainability impacts.

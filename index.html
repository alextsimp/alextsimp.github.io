About

With over 8 years of experience in energy systems and computational fluid dynamics, I specialize in developing high-performance computational codes using FORTRAN, Python, and MATLAB. My work bridges the gap between complex numerical methods and sustainable engineering applications.

I actively coordinate technical activities for EU Horizon projects and am a published author of 10+ peer-reviewed articles, including 7 as first author. From modeling thermal storage systems to executing simulation of aerosol flows, my focus remains on utilizing engineering methodologies to drive positive environmental impacts.
